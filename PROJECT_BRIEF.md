# Project Brief: JagoCurhat

## Overview

JagoCurhat is a mobile-first social platform for sharing emotions, stories, thoughts, and anonymous confessions.

The app is inspired by Threads but focuses on:
- Emotional comfort
- Safe sharing
- Minimalism
- Cute aesthetics
- Low social pressure
- Anonymous expression

The platform should feel:
- Calming
- Empathetic
- Expressive
- Cozy
- Emotionally safe
- Playful but not childish
